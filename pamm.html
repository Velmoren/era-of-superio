What Are PAMM Accounts?

The Percentage Allocation Management Module (PAMM) is a Forex trading solution that allows investors to pool their funds with a professional trader, known as a Money Manager. The Money Manager executes trades on behalf of the investors, aiming to generate profits for all participants.

Advantages of PAMM Accounts

Potential for High Earnings

Skilled Money Managers leverage pooled funds to increase trade success rates, boosting potential profitability.

Superior Pricing and Execution:

Enjoy tight spreads, minimal latency, and quick trade execution, ensuring optimal trading performance.

Robust Technology

Advanced systems provide stable server uptime, seamless functionality, and a user-friendly experience.

Customizable Accounts

Tailor trading conditions, such as spreads, commissions, and performance fees, to suit specific needs.

Advantages for Managers:
  • Utilize expertise to increase income
  • Earnings depend on performance and attracted capital
  • Unlimited investors with customizable investment terms
  • Simple trading on a single account without complex accounting
Advantages for Investors:
  • Pay managers only when profits are made
  • Earn without being a trading expert
  • Funds are secure—only the investor can withdraw
  • Choose from hundreds of managers and diversify investments
